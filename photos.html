Melodies of Life

We are so blessed with the generosity of our loving mother Earth: breath-taking nature, innocent animals, adored people, mystical cities and many others that you can name them all. It is our privilege to enjoy every single spark of the Creation, along with our duty to protect all these precious gems for the future generations.

These are collection of charming moments I have captured around the world. I am sure you will like them. Please relax and enjoy!

Beautiful sunset in Vancouver summer. The color was so unreal!!! For a moment I felt like this is almost like a heaven on Earth.

A bright corner in Toronto. Truly awesome, reminds me of the old busy days in Singapore and Japan.

Tourny Fountain in Quebec. Cannot describe how magnificent it is. I spent lots of time just to adore this majestic piece of art.

This is not a photoshop picture, truly! It is the Emerald lake in Yoho National Park. I drove more than 12 hours just to see this colorful gem (first!).

Rome, particularity Vatican is one of the most picturesque city that I have ever been to. Love the golden tone and grand design of the historical building.

Saw these beautiful miko (shrine maidens) while wandering around Miyajima Island. They were so gorgeous even from afar!

Esplanade is the best spot in Singapore to take pictures at night. From this angle is Raffles Place - the sparkling financial district where I used to work for years.
